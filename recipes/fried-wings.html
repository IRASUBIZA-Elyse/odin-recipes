French-fries

Best dish ever with less knowledge and few minutes to prepare it.

Ingredients

  • chicken wings
  • cooking oil
  • salt
  • egg mixture
  • flour mixture

Steps involved in preparation

  1. dip chicken in egg mixture
  2. placein the flour mixture
  3. heat the cooking oil
  4. fry the wings when oil is well heated for 7-8 minutes
  5. when the chicken is gold remove it cz it's ready
